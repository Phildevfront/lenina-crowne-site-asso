About us

Influenced by bands like Hot Water Music, Iron Chic or
Samiam, Lenina Crowne navigates since 2008 between power rock and emo punk.

After 2 Eps, a self-produced album, and many first parts
(Uncommonmenfrommars, Seven Hate, Dead Pop Club, Justin(e), Burning Heads,
Mad Caddies…), the group returns to the best of its form with a new
formation and a new EP "Duality", which combines energy and unstoppable harmonies.

Formed at the end of 2008 around former Crastination / Burning Each Day / Skaouf / Sheemshill,
Lenina Crowne condenses, at first, influences that are both garage,
post-punk and metal.
Since its beginnings and more than fifty concerts to its credit, the group arrives
finally to a sound of its own without "denying" the slightest bit of its influences
“varied” on the punk-rock scene
